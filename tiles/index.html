ROCKSTAR LIFE
Men in suits in the lounge in a Californian home: presenting A$AP Rocky, Iggy Pop and Tyler, The Creator in the new Gucci Tailoring campaign.
Rock stars at home inspires the images and video featuring the artists with their pets, including Iggy Pop’s parrot Biggy Pop and a blue hyacinth macaw.
